ABOUT US
kal is one of the top destinations in juba. A must visit natural sanctuary, a place where you will find a connection to nature. Here you will discover and enjoy nature in lots of different and unique ways. kal, definitely will be one of them. kal is an exclusive mountain hideaway, immerse within nature on a privileged location. The kal hotel has luxurious accommodations and great facilities for a perfect stay. The kal property is wide and spacious, full of nature and tropical gardens; with 9 hectares of land, and spacious rooms, here, you will be able to just relax and enjoy juba on this elegant lodge. The rooms are cozy, yet elegant, spacious and comfortable with large windows that help bring the outdoors inside. You will have all the commodities so you can simply relax, and recharge energies after a full day of adventures in kal. At kal you will find everything necessary to enjoy a perfect stay at in juba. Great rooms with all commodities, amazing food at our restaurant, perfect entertainment at our sports bar, you will be able to relax at our hot tubs after a day of adventures or take a swim on our heated pool. kal definitely is a hotel like no other in juba.
